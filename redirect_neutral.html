Assignment Brief
Welcome to Jones & Jones

You are a junior marketing strategist at Jones & Jones, one of the city's most competitive agencies. Today, your four-person team is pitching ARIA — a smart home device that combines cutting-edge technology with a commitment to sustainability.

The pitch is in 15 minutes, and your team leader will be delivering it. Your leader has divided the work across the team, and every member's contribution counts.

Your Assignment — Sustainability

Consumers today do not simply want smart devices — they want devices that align with their values. ARIA delivers on that promise: energy-efficient, manufactured with recycled materials, and designed to reduce household waste over time.

Your task is to craft 2–3 taglines that bring this story to life. Consider what would compel a sustainability-conscious consumer to stop and take notice.

Examples for reference:
"Power your home. Protect the planet."
"Smart living, smaller footprint."

Once submitted, your contributions will be compiled into the team's shared pitch document. Your leader will review all submissions before the team finalizes the pitch.
